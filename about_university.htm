About University


Facilities on campus are extensive and provide much more than just an academic experience. They include:

  • Sports center

  • University Gallery, which, as well as supporting local artists, and the Lakeside Theatre,

  • A wide variety of bars and restaurants

  • The Careers Centre

Further facilities include:

  • Banks (Lloyds, Barclays, Santander)

  • Employability and Careers Centre

  • Computing Service

  • Day Nursery

  • Library and study facilities

  • Students' Union

  • The Multi-Faith Chaplaincy Centre

  • The Health Centre

  • Greenstead House (Pharmacy)

  • Information Office (University guards and security)

  • Advice Centre: Advice on finance, applications, course issue, contraception, safety, and many other issues.

  • Support Centre: Students find support for personal, academic and any other kind of support by university stuff.
