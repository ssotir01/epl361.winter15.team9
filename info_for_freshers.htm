Here you can find anything you need to know about your arrival and settling in Essex University.

 

Airports in London:

London is served by five main airports: Heathrow, Stansted, Gatwick, Luton and London City. The most convenient one for Essex Cypriot Students is the Stansted Airport.

                           

What will freshers need for their arrival at ESSEX.

Passport, Plane ticket, Offer letter, Original examination, Certificates (true copies),  Student loan confirmation letter/copy of the application, Accommodation letter

 

General Information:

  • The period for receiving the keys for the campus accommodation at the University of Essex is 27 Sep – 2 Oct

  • Freshers can collect their keys from 9am-until late.

  • First payment for the accommodation must be received by 31 Oct.

  • During freshers week freshers’ information point will be on Square 4.

  • The registration will be during freshers week and every department has a specific date for the registration and that date will be given to students according to the department he/she belongs. (Freshers should bring with them the acceptance letters and all the certificates that they sent with their application to the university).

 

Opening a new bank account:

There are several banks in Colchester where you can open a new bank account but in University’s Campus there are only Lloyds, Barclays and Santander.

To open your new bank account you will need an identity card or a passport and a bank letter from the university where you can order it from the registry office or you can order it online at https://www.essex.ac.uk/studentdocs/ and you will get it after 5 working days.

Bus Card:

Eyes wide open Freshers! During the Freshers week you can find in Square 3 and 4 different stands where you can buy your Bus and Rail card. A Bus card costs 160 pounds and a Rail card 28 pounds. The cards are valid for one year. The bus card provides you with unlimited access to bus routes around Colchester.
